British Touring Car Championship Challenge

Years: 2011–2020 Front-engined Weight Max: 1350 kg

Chassis & Body

  • Panel material: Steel, treated steel, partial aluminium, or aluminium allowed; carbon‑fibre panels banned.
  • Chassis type: Monocoque only.
  • Chassis material: Any steel or aluminium‑based monocoque material is allowed. Carbon‑fibre monocoque is banned.
  • Engine placement: Must be a front engine layout (longitudinal or transverse); no mid‑ or rear‑engined.
  • Suspension geometry: Front: MacPherson Strut or Double Wishbone only. Rear: Semi‑Trailing Arm, Multi‑Link, or Double Wishbone only. No solid axles, torsion beam, pushrod, or leaf‑spring setups.

Engine

  • Engine layout: Inline‑4 only. Any head/valve system is allowed (SOHC/DOHC, 4v/5v, with or without VVL) as long as it’s realistic for the year.
  • Displacement: Total capacity between 1.95 L and 2.00 L.
  • Aspiration: Single turbocharger only (no superchargers, no twin‑turbo setups, no naturally aspirated engines.) Intercooler allowed.
  • Power window: Finished engine power between 320 hp and 380 hp.
  • Fuel system: Fuel injection, if Direct Injection is unlocked for your year, you run Direct Injection.
  • RPM & reliability: Rev limit between 6 500 rpm and 8 000 rpm; engine reliability must be at least green in the stats.
  • Free for creativity: Bore vs stroke, compression, cam profile, VVT/VVL choices, turbo size/boost curve, exhaust design and mufflers.

Drivetrain

  • Drive type: FWD or RWD only; AWD and 4x4 are banned.
  • Gearbox type: 6‑speed Sequential gearbox is mandatory. No manuals, autos or more than 6 gears.
  • Ratios: Top‑speed slider must give an estimated top speed between 135–155 mph. You're free to choose spacing, but make sure the car actually hits redline in top gear on the gearing graph.
  • Differential: Must use an LSD. Any LSD type is fine, viscous, geared, electronic. Open and locked differentials are banned.

Wheels, Tyres & Brakes

  • Rim diameter: 18" rims mandatory front and rear.
  • Rim material: Alloy or Magnesium only; no Steel and no Carbon‑fibre rims.
  • Tyre type: Must be Radial. Dry compound: Semi Slicks only.
  • Tyre widths: Front width between 235 mm and 265 mm. Rear width between 225 mm and 265 mm. Front and rear must be within 20 mm of each other (e.g., 245/245, 255/245 etc.) no drag‑style huge rear or tiny front tyres.
  • Brake type: Disc brakes on all four corners. Front: Vented Discs only. Rear: Solid or Vented Discs allowed. Drum brakes are banned; Carbon‑ceramic discs are banned.
  • Brake sizing & pads: Front diameter must be 300–380 mm; rear 260–340 mm, sized so brake fade stays green in graphs. Pad slider must be between 40 and 80 (aggressive race pads.)

Aero & Cooling

  • Undertray: Must be Clad type undertray.
  • Active aero: Disabled, no active wings or flaps.
  • Wings / lips / splitters: You may use front lips/splitters and a single main rear wing or spoiler from the Fixtures. Wing angle sliders must stay in the lower two‑thirds of their range at both ends (no maxed‑out airplane wings).
  • Cooling & brake airflow: Engine cooling slider must be between 60 and 100% of required cooling. You can’t under‑cool the engine for free drag. Brake airflow slider between 10 and 40 to limit fade.

Interior, Safety & Electronics

  • Seats & interior: 1 or 2 seats only. Interior must be “Basic” or “Sport". Entertainment: “None” or “Basic” only.
  • Safety: Use the highest safety level unlocked for your chosen year.
  • Steering & driver aids: Steering: Any hydraulic power steering only. All electronic driver aids must be OFF: no ABS, no Traction Control, no ESC.

Suspension

  • Spring type: Standard or Progressive springs only; no Hydropneumatic or Air springs.
  • Dampers: Gas Mono‑Tube or Adaptive allowed; Semi‑Active and full. Active dampers are banned to keep it analogue.
  • Sway bars: Normal (passive) or sport bars only; no active/semiactive sway bars.
  • Ride height: Final ride height must be low enough that the car does not bottom out, but not slammed.
  • Tuning: You're free to choose exact spring rates, damper values, sway bar stiffness and camber.
